East Asia and China

The East Asian Realm

  • Area of 4.5 million square miles
  • Population: 1.666 Billion (2018)
    • It is the second most populous region in the world after Southern Asia
  • Other countries in East Asia include: Mongolia, North Korea, South Korea and Japan

Map of East Asia

China is the world's fourth largest country and occupies the majority of land in East Asia. It is the world's most populous country. Its population is about 1.38 billion (2017) and its area is 3.7 million square miles. It is bordered by 14 neighboring countries including India and Pakistan.

Beijing

  • Beijing's location---Latitude: 39° 54' 27.00'' N, Longitude: 116° 23' 50.03'' E (GPS coordinates of Beijing).
  • It is China's capital and has a population of almost 20 million (CIA Fact book, China).

China's Topography

  • A great portion of the country is covered by mountains and deserts
  • The Himalayas cover the country's south eastern boundary
  • China's has deserts to its north (the Gobi and Taklamkan)
  • The country's main rivers run west to east: Huang(Yellow), Yangzi, and the Xi Jinag

Ethnicities

  • 90% of the population is Han Chinese
  • Mandarin is the official language
  • Bhuddism is common in this region
  • The remaining 10% of the population belong to 45 distinct nationalities each with their own language
  • This 10% is mostly in the western 'autonomous' regions

Population Distribution

  • Most of China's population lives close to the coastal region (15% of the countries area)--very dense
  • As you travel toward the west the population density thins out
